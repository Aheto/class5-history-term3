1. Overview

Mini OpenStax is a client-side, offline-first educational platform designed for Ghanaian JHS students. Unlike traditional learning apps, we do not collect personal data, use tracking cookies, or store information on remote servers. Your learning experience happens entirely on your device.

Core Principle: If it's not on your device, it doesn't exist. We prioritize student privacy by design.

2. What Data We Store

We only store the following information locally on your browser/device using localStorage:

  • Display Name: Used only to personalize greetings (e.g., "Welcome, Kwame!").
  • Role Preference: "Student" or "Instructor" mode toggle.
  • Progress Tracking: Lesson mastery status, quiz attempt counts, and timestamps.
  • Quiz Reports: Generated locally when you complete a quiz. Stored only until you delete them or clear browser data.

We do not collect: IP addresses, device identifiers, location data, browsing history, or analytics events.

3. How We Use Data

All stored data serves a single purpose: enhancing your personal learning experience.

  • Personalization: Greets you by name and adapts UI based on your selected role.
  • Progress Tracking: Shows mastery indicators, progress bars, and analytics in the Dashboard.
  • Report Generation: Compiles quiz results into shareable text files for WhatsApp or PDF export.
  • Offline Continuity: Allows you to resume lessons without an internet connection.

No data is transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly initiate a share action (e.g., clicking "Send Report via WhatsApp").

4. LocalStorage & Offline Function

Modern browsers use localStorage to save small amounts of data directly on your device. Mini OpenStax uses this to:

  1. Remember your name and preferences between visits.
  2. Track which lessons you've completed and your quiz scores.
  3. Generate reports that remain accessible even offline.

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's "Site Data" or "LocalStorage" in settings. Doing so will reset progress and preferences but will not affect other websites.

5. Third-Party Services

Mini OpenStax loads only two external resources to improve visual quality:

  • Google Fonts: Loads the Inter & Playfair Display typefaces. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Font Awesome (CDN): Loads icons for navigation and UI elements.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, ad networks, or any tracking scripts. WhatsApp links open your native app with pre-filled text; no data is sent through our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

This platform is specifically designed for Junior High School students (ages 12–15) in Ghana. We comply with global children's privacy standards (COPPA, GDPR-K) by:

  • Collecting zero identifiable personal information.
  • Requiring no account creation, email verification, or phone numbers.
  • Displaying no advertisements or external tracking.
  • Storing all progress locally on the user's device.

Parents and educators can safely allow students to use Mini OpenStax without privacy concerns.

7. Your Rights & Controls

You maintain full control over your data:

  • Access: View your stored data via browser developer tools (Application → LocalStorage).
  • Export: Use the "Export Analytics" or "Send Report via WhatsApp" buttons to save your progress.
  • Delete: Clear browser data to permanently erase all Mini OpenStax progress.
  • Opt-Out: Use the platform fully offline after initial load; no data sync is required.
