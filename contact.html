Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions from parents, teachers, and students.

Is Mini OpenStax really free to use?
Yes! Mini OpenStax is completely free for all Ghanaian learners, parents, and teachers. Our content is aligned with the NaCCA standards-based curriculum and is designed to be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.
Which classes and subjects do you cover?
We currently offer comprehensive lesson plans and interactive content for Primary school classes, including English Language, Mathematics, Our World Our People, History, RME, and Creative Arts. Content is organised by class and term to match the Ghanaian academic calendar.
How do I track my child's progress?
Each lesson includes a built-in Dashboard where learners can see their quiz scores, mastered topics, and recent activity. Parents can also receive progress reports directly via WhatsApp after each quiz. Teachers can switch to Instructor Mode in Settings to view class-wide analytics.
Do I need an internet connection to use the lessons?
An internet connection is needed to load the lesson pages initially. However, once loaded, the quizzes and dashboard work offline because your progress is saved locally in your browser. We're working on a fully offline mobile app for areas with limited connectivity.
Is the content aligned with the NaCCA curriculum?
Absolutely. All our lesson plans, learning indicators, and assessment items are carefully aligned with the National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (NaCCA) standards-based curriculum for Ghana. Teachers can confidently use our materials in the classroom.
How can I become a partner or contributor?
We welcome partnerships with schools, NGOs, educational organisations, and individual contributors. Please use the contact form above and select "Partner / Organisation" as your role, or email us directly at learn@miniopenstax.edu.gh.
Can I print the lesson notes for offline use?
Yes! Every lesson page is print-friendly. Simply use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P) to save the lesson as a PDF or print it directly. Quiz reports can also be exported as PDFs from the Dashboard.
How do I report a bug or suggest an improvement?
We appreciate your feedback! Use the contact form above with the subject "Bug Report" or "Suggestion", or reach out via WhatsApp. Please include the lesson name, what you were trying to do, and what went wrong. Screenshots are very helpful.
